About Us

Established in 2004, GNK Prints & Designs has been at the forefront of the graphic design industry, continuously pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation. We are a team of passionate designers, artists, and craftsmen who thrive on transforming your ideas into visually stunning, tangible products.
Our mission is simple - to bring your ideas to life with creativity, precision, and unparalleled quality. Services

Screen Printing
Our state-of-the-art screen printing facility allows us to produce high-quality prints on various materials, from apparel to promotional products. Whether you need custom t-shirts for an event or branded merchandise for your business, we have the expertise to make it happen.
Digital Printing
Harnessing the power of cutting-edge digital printing technology, we can reproduce your designs with unmatched clarity and vibrancy. From posters to banners, our digital printing services are ideal for both small and large-scale projects.
Digital Embroidery
Add a touch of sophistication and elegance to your garments and accessories with our digital embroidery services. Our skilled artisans can embroider your logo or design onto clothing, hats, and more, creating a lasting impression of your brand.
